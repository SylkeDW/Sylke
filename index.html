Work, work, work

After high school, I did a bachelor's degree in medical laboratory. I started working as lab technician in a clinical lab of a hospital but after a few years I wanted a job with some more responsibilities. I transferred in the same hospital to the pathalogical lab, where I started as main lab technician combined with the position of quality analyst. In 2018, after a fusion with another lab, I only did the job of quality analyst. I liked doing my job, but after all these years of working at a lab, I started looking forward to a different type of job. Hence the switch to funcional analyst.
